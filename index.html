About Me

Creative Professional & Production Expert

Passionate About Visual Storytelling

I'm a Production Manager, Artist, and Voice Artist with hands-on experience across a wide range of visual content from advertisement films and corporate videos to social media campaigns and industrial shoots.

I enjoy being part of the entire process, from shaping an idea to seeing it come alive on screen. With a strong understanding of both the creative and production sides, I focus on turning concepts into engaging visual stories while keeping everything on set running smoothly.

My background in acting and voice work gives me a different perspective during production I naturally connect with performance, timing, and emotion, which helps bring more depth to the final output.

I believe good storytelling is what truly connects with people and makes a brand memorable.

Alongside my work, I'm constantly refining my craft in cinematography and lighting, spending time with my own gear to better understand visuals and improve my eye for detail.
